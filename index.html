“Music is a language that doesn’t speak in particular words. It speaks in emotions, and if it’s in the bones, it’s in the bones.”

Our main goals include the promotion of young musical talent, the integration of musicians with immigrant backgrounds, and the dissemination of music to society.

We present before you, 'NEEHAR' the product of sincere effort from a group of music loving people. Since our baby steps in the world of music as 'OLAM', we have received support and love from our audience. Due unforeseen causes, our name had to be changed. With everyone staying home and quarantining we've decided on bringing a small treat for everyone. Be on the lookout for fun content!
Keep supporting us and your blessing is of utmost value to our humble initiative.
